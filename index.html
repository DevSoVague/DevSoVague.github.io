About Me

               
               

                                            I am a passionate and driven engineer at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence** and **Biomedical Engineering**. Currently pursuing my Master's in AI Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, I thrive on tackling complex, real-world problems by leveraging data.                                                                 My journey from a B.Tech in Bioengineering at IIT Jodhpur has been defined by a deep-seated curiosity for understanding the "why" and "how"—from analyzing genomic data on HPC clusters to understand Parkinson's, to developing low-latency BCI systems for assistive robotics. I am always eager to collaborate on projects that push the boundaries of technology to create a tangible impact on human health.
