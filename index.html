About Me

Welcome! I'm Devavrath Sandeep, an engineer passionate about the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Biomedical Engineering. My goal is to use technology to create a tangible impact on human health.

I am an engineer, currently pursuing a Master's in AI Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, I have a background in Bioengineering from IIT Jodhpur.

My passion lies in tackling complex challenges, whether it's building low-latency BCI systems for assistive robotics or analyzing large-scale genomic data on HPC clusters. I'm driven by a deep curiosity to understand the "why" and "how" behind the data to find solutions that can help people.

I thrive on finding solutions in data and am always open to discussing new projects, research, or collaborations.
